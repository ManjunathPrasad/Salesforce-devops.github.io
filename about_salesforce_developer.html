Salesforce Developer Roles and Responsibilities

Salesforce Developer Roles:

  • Developing customized solutions within the Salesforce platform.
  • Designing, coding, and implementing Salesforce applications.
  • Customizing Salesforce to meet specific business needs.
  • Integrating Salesforce with other systems.
  • Creating and maintaining technical documentation.
  • Providing support and troubleshooting for Salesforce applications.
  • Participating in code reviews and ensuring best practices are followed.

Salesforce Developer Responsibilities:

  • Collaborating with stakeholders to gather requirements and understand business needs.
  • Translating business requirements into technical solutions.
  • Developing and maintaining Apex code, Visualforce pages, and Lightning components.
  • Building and maintaining integrations using REST, SOAP, and other APIs.
  • Performing unit testing and debugging to ensure code quality and stability.
  • Keeping up-to-date with Salesforce updates and best practices.
  • Participating in the deployment process and ensuring smooth rollouts.
